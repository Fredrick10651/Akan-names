AKAN NAMES

In Ghana children are given there first their first name as a 'day name' which corresponds to the day in the week they were born.This names are called Akan Names.

These names are also used among Ghanaians living abroad and among Africans living in the diaspora who wish to identify with their ancestral homeland.

This application is to help you know the day of the week you were born and your Akan Name corresponding to your day and your gender.

Please fill in your date of bith, month and year to this form to know your Akan name
